‚ùì How to Use

This is your personal TaL TierList Maker!

On Desktop:
1. Use the text field to search for movies/series.
2. **Drag & Drop** posters between Tiers (SSS to C) or to the "Movies & Series" area below. (Can be disabled in settings to use **Click-to-Move** instead.)
3. Upload your own images using "Choose Files".
4. "Save as Image" to download your finished list.
5. "Save Current List" to save your progress in the browser.

On Mobile:
1. **Tap on a poster** to open a menu and move it to a different Tier.
2. All other functions (search, upload, save, reset) work the same.
